I'm a Business Economics student at UC Riverside with a 3.8 GPA, and I have a passion for people, brands, and building meaningful experiences. Whether I'm in the classroom, on the soccer field, or working, I bring the same energy to everything I do. I am determined, focused, dependable, and genuinely invested in doing things well because for me, excellent results aren't just a goal, they are a standard.

My professional journey started early. For over six years, I worked as a clerk at Campitelli Cookies and Sandwiches, gaining valuable hands-on experience. From handling customer service and managing transactions to maintaining food quality standards and keeping things running smoothly as part of a tight-knit team. Every day was an opportunity to grow. It was the kind of role that taught me how to show up consistently with a good attitude, communicate clearly, and take pride in the small details that make a big difference in someone's day. Those years shaped my understanding of what great customer experience actually looks like from the inside out. Alongside that, I've spent years babysitting for families in the area, building long-term relationships rooted in trust, structure, and care. I managed schedules, prepared meals, helped with homework, and created environments where kids could thrive. It sounds simple, but it required a level of independence, emotional intelligence, and reliability that I carry into everything I do professionally. Families trusted me with what matters most to them, and I never took that lightly. As a Division I NCAA soccer player at UC Riverside, I've learned what it truly means to balance competing priorities without compromising anything. Managing a rigorous academic schedule alongside the demands of collegiate athletics has sharpened my time management, my resilience, and my ability to stay composed and perform under pressure. Being part of a high-performance team has also reinforced something I genuinely believe: the best results come from people who trust each other, communicate openly, and hold each other accountable. That culture of excellence on the field is something I bring into every environment I'm part of.

Outside of school and sport, I'm drawn to the world of content creation, wellness, and brand storytelling. I'm fluent across platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn, and I have a natural eye for visual presentation and understanding how brands connect with their audiences in authentic ways. I'm especially interested in how lifestyle, nutrition, and wellness brands build community and communicate their values, as it's an intersection that feels very aligned with who I am. I find myself constantly thinking about how brands show up, what they stand for, and how that translates into real connection with people. I'm currently in my final stretch at UCR and actively looking for opportunities where I can bring my business background, creative instincts, and tireless work ethic to a team doing something they're proud of. I thrive in fast-paced, people-centered environments where growth is encouraged, and hard work is recognized. If that sounds like your team, I'd love to connect.
